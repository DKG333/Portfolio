About me

Hi my name is Dean and I'm currently a Game Developer in the Greater Toronto Area I;m really passionate about making great games for people to enjoy . It all started for me when I enrolled at triOS College for Game Development and Design, it was the best decision i ever made. It was an intense course, i spent and year and a half learning everything you need to know on how make a game from scratch. During that time I've learned a lot. Not only did it give me the knowledge about making video games but it also taught me how to deal with challenge's and how to push through the Grind. I went from knowing nothing about coding or designing video games to making some cool fun games for everyone to play. I hope you enjoy playing them just as much i had making them. Do not hesitate to ask any questions. i also have a dedicated blog explaining my entire experience at triOS College. Feel to click on the icon for WordPress and check it out, as you scroll down you can check out my work that I've done myself and created with other classmates. We put together some pretty fun enjoyable games.
